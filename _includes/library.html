Books

Delivering Happiness: A Path to Profits, Passion, and Purpose
by Tony Hsieh

After reading this book, I understand that how important the customer is. And the company culture and value have a huge impact on the whole company. It let me think that when I am considering joining a company, the culture of the company is crucial for me.


Remote: Office Not Required
by David Heinemeier Hansson, Jason Fried

I am interested in remote work all the time. This book told me that how remote team can work efficiently and how remote work can go on well. This book helped me work with my clients who is far from me. It also let me know how amazing the remote work is.


How to Win Friends and Influence People
by Dale Carnegie

I learned how to treat people kind and honest through this book. It changed my perspective in my life and make me more positive and happy. I read this book many times.


UX for Lean Startups
by Laura Klein

I learned lots of methods that how to quickly validate the MVP, the market and the problem I assume from this book. It also has many resources about how to conduct user research in lean.
