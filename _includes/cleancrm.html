Clean CRM

Management product for cleaning service companies. Smoothly administrating all the customers, bookings, quotes and employees on a cleaning service company based in NYC.

For both small and big businesses, sharing and managing work within teams is still too difficult. This is far from solved into a so competitive industry as the Cleaning Services one. Companies loose track of their employees, clients and time. Putting their businesses at the end of the trail.

Putting togheter an easy and pleasing interface, with the CRM system, leaderboard and detailed client profiles, cleaners and administrators can now easily identify pending, past works and reach out to their costumers.
