For both small and big businesses, sharing and managing work within teams is still too complicated. This is far from solved into such a competitive industry as the Cleaning Services one. Companies lose track of their employees, clients and time, Settling their businesses at the end of the trail.

Putting together a comfortable and pleasing interface, with the CRM system, leaderboard and detailed client profiles, cleaners and administrators can now quickly identify pending, past works and reach out to their customers.
