We had a problem; the client told about the need of some brand design for a new company she wanted to start. We sat down and brainstormed ideas and services that were simple enough to launch without technical skills.

The client and me, tried to be effective during our research phase, making it short and useful. We gathered data from user feedback, reports, and competitive analysis. We continued to create assumptions that we later validated with surveys and interviews of the staff.

THE PROCESS

We decided to design and developed a quick new website, in order to test the business idea. The site serves as a point of trust and marketing, creating interest for developers to apply and clients to work with us.
